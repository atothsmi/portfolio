Alia Toth-Smith

Who I Am

Alia Toth-Smith is a writer, designer, and programmer currently pursuing a B.A. in Art and Design:Games and Playable Media at University of California, Santa Cruz. She uses her creative, technical, and analytical tools to produce creative projects in a variety of genres. She's currently exploring sound design, table top role-playing games, and short, story-based games.

In her free time, she likes to play with her dog, write fiction, and spend time with her family.

In Spring 2021, she worked as an assistant director on the French play for the Miriam Ellis International Playhouse.
