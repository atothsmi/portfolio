Extinction Party (Spring 2022)

A satirical take on animal extinction awareness.
Browse through to learn how ten distinct animals went extinct.

Created in conjunction with four other team members, she was responsible for all interactions on this website. She also contributed heavily to the CSS and overall theme of the website. During group meetings, she led discussion, delegated work, and helped her teammates complete their portions.

Made for ART 101 at UCSC taught by Wes Modes.

The Last Heart (Winter 2022)

In Nequam Mountain the undead seeks to rise again. Warriors from the past have left items to aid you quest, but every boon has its cost. Will you be able to defeat the enemy within and save your people?

For this project, the team wanted to create an extremely unforgiving hack-and-slash RPG. She was responsible for coordinating all group members by creating a development plan, running meetings, and keeping track of tasks to complete. She also contributed to level design, programming, story, and other assorted art.

Other Credits:
Engine: Construct3, free version

Made for CMPM 80k at UCSC taught by Tad Leckman and Noah Wardrip-Fruin

The World Moves On (Fall 2021)

What do you do after something unbearable happens to someone you love?
How can you help if they're stuck in the past?

In this interactive narrative, she wanted to look at loss and depression from an outside perspective. She worked in grayscale to set the tone and used text placement and font to differentiate between each voice.

The Unfortunate Tail of Fancy the Cat (Fall 2021)

A story about a cat that always ends horribly.

This branching narrative aims to explore surface level success and an unhealthy obsession with work. In it, she played with a contrast between a silly visual aesthetic and a dark topic to make her point.
