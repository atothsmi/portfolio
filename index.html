Extinction Party

A satirical take on animal extinction awareness, browse through to learn more on how ten distinct animals went extinct, each with a common denominator (a.k.a human activity).

Created in conjunction with a team with four other members, Alia was responsible for all interactions on this website. She also contributed heavily to the CSS and overall theme of the website. During group meetings, she lead discussion, delegated work, and helped her teammates with their portions.

This website was made for ART 101 at UCSC taught by Wes Modes.

About Me

Hi! My name is Alia Toth-Smith. I am a writer and programmer. I'm currently pursuing a Bachelors of Arts in Art & Design: Games & Playable Media (AGPM). I grew up in the Bay Area. In my free time, I like to write fiction, sing, read DND books, and play with my dog, Charlotte.
