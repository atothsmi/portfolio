About Me

Alia Toth-Smith is a writer, designer, and programmer currently pursuing a B.A. in Art and Design:Games and Playable Media at University of California, Santa Cruz. She uses her creative, technical, and analytical tools to produce creative projects in a variety of genres.

In her free time, she likes to play with her dog, write fiction, and spend time with her family In Spring 2021, she worked as an assistant director on the French play for the Miriam Ellis International Playhouse.
