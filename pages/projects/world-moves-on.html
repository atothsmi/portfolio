The World Moves On

Fall 2021, Narrative Designer and Visual Designer

This interactive narrative looks from the perspective of a friend at someone who's losing themselves to depression and loss. It's all done in grayscale to set the tone and the colors of each choice communicate some of their subtext. Each voice is differentiated through text placement and font. Time in-between responses is also used to replicate the natural (or unintended or purposeful) pauses in conversation.


Remixed on Glitch from a Program made by Adam Smith

Made for CMPM 35 at UCSC taught by Adam Smith
