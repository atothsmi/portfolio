Extinction Party

Spring 2022

This project was designed to raise awareness on human's role in extinction. It features 10 animals that are already extinct due to human actions and ends with a call to action that references other endangered animals.

This website was created with a team, credited below. I functioned as the primary programmer and project manager. I was responsible for all of the interactions on the website. I also contributed to the theme of the website and the CSS design. We met several times over the course of three months (one quarter of university). During those meetings, I lead discussion on the project, delegated work, and helped teammates troubleshoot their tasks when needed.

While this website is certainly imperfect, it was an excellent exercise in website design and programming with javascript. It also provided several insights into how to work as a group and how to delegate tasks while balancing everyone's strengths and weaknesses.
